Highlights
  • All highlight and header text will use Rogue Script font family.
  • Highlight and header text will be displayed at a size of 64px with a font weight of normal.
  • All highlight sections will have a background color of #00796B and a text color of #FFFFFF.
  • Heading script will have a margin and padding of 0px.
  • Varying levels of background transparency are allowed to accomodate background images.
      • Body Text
  • All body text will use the Georgia font family.
  • Body text will be displayed at a size of 16px with a font weight of normal.
  • All body sections will have a background color of #009688 and a text color of #FFFFFF.
  • Body script will have a margin and padding of 0px.
  • Varying levels of background transparency are allowed to accomodate background images.
      • Data Text
  • All data text will use the Tahoma font family.
  • Data text will be displayed at a size of 16px with a font weight of normal.
  • All bodylight sections will have a background color of #B2DFDB and a text color of #212121.
  • Heading script will have a margin and padding of 0px.
  • Varying levels of transparency is allowed to accomodate background images.

    • #00796B

    This color will be used for primary header sections with white text.

    #009688

    This color will be used for primary data sections with #212121 for text.

    #212121
